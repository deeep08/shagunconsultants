About Us

What makes us different from others?

Shagun Consultants is a company specialised in interiors design architecture and have been successfully providing architectural services for the last 11 years. All our projects are results of correct combination of creativity, functionality, originality and innovative design.

We create aesthetic and function space for living, working and making business according to the personality and specific need that each ambiance requires. Our Organisation support on a team of designers, architectures, consultants, professionals, graphic designers, decorators and any necessary to develop the best project for our client.

Shagun Consultants India has managed several private as well as public sector. We provide sustainable and environmentally responsible interior design and architecture without compromising on aesthetic. We create sustainable healthy space.

Our Team works with flexibility and highly qualified team of architects and interior designers.
